Tanjiro Kamado

A Pure Heart

Tanjiro Kamado, a fitctional character in Demon Slayer:Kimetsu no Yaiba is a virtue, honest and humble boy. He exhibits a great of determination and will not give up so easily regardless the risk of the situation.

  • At seventeen, he was a swordsman from the beginning. He received assistance from his teacher Sakonji Urokodaki, and as a result, he now has abilities beyond those of his fellow Hashira members.
  • Tanjiro have two unique Breathing techniques: water breathing and sunlight breathing
  • Tanjiro has a hard skull he inherited from his mother. His head is very durable to the point in a single headbutt, it's physically strong enough to cause immediate damage to his opponents
  • Tanjiro developed recovery breathing that allows him to recover from fatigue and stiff muscles that prevent his body from moving, which he uses to overcome the whiplash of using Hinokami Kagura techniques twice in a row.

  • Tanjiro has incredible stamina and endurance, even before he became a Demon Slayer.

    This showed when he had to train in a mountain will little to no oxygen for many days, dodging booby traps, running and traversing through it up and down, and improving his swordsmanship skills and physique all while under these harsh conditions

  • Tanjiro possesses a sense of smell bordering on superhuman territory.

    Even before becoming a Demon Slayer,he is able to pick up even the most subtle of scents and track their location with terrifying accuracy over long distances.
